The Science Behind Hook & Loop Tapes

Invented in the 1940s and inspired by nature, hook & loop tape consists of two components: the hook side (tiny stiff hooks) and the loop side (softer loops). When pressed together, they create a strong, flexible, and reusable bond—ideal for fast, secure, and damage-free fastening.
