Gripnex – Reinforced Nylon Hook & Loop Tape

Next-Gen Fastening. Zero Compromise Gripnex is Walonium’s advanced reinforced nylon hook & loop tape, specifically designed for high-stress, high-performance environments. Crafted with a rugged weave of industrial-strength nylon, Gripnex offers maximum tensile strength and long-lasting durability—making it the ideal choice for aerospace, defense, tactical gear, automotive interiors, and industrial applications. It resists extreme temperatures, heavy loads, and repeated use without losing grip integrity.

Built for Demanding Applications

Unlike standard hook & loop systems, Gripnex is reinforced to handle the toughest operational conditions. It delivers superior holding power even in vibration-prone or mobile environments, ensuring safety, stability, and reliability. Whether it's for securing components in aircraft interiors, heavy-duty gear in military kits, or fastening parts in automotive systems, Gripnex delivers performance with zero compromise.

Specifications & Customization

  • Material: Reinforced Industrial Nylon
  • Temperature Resistance: -20°C to 120°C
  • Closure Strength: Extra high tensile & shear strength
  • Cycle Life: 10,000+ openings/closings
  • Resistant to: Moisture, UV, oil, and wear
  • Available Widths: 20mm to 100mm
  • Color Options: Standard black, custom colors on request
  • Packaging: Rolls, pre-cuts, or die-cuts based on requirement
