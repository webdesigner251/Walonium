Suregrip - Hook and Loop Tape

Walonium's Suregrip Soft Touch Velcro Tape is designed to deliver a smooth, low-profile fastening solution that’s gentle on delicate fabrics and sensitive materials. Unlike standard hook & loop systems, this tape features a softer loop and micro hook design, making it ideal for apparel, baby products, medical garments, sportswear, and upholstery where skin contact and comfort are key.

Ideal for Sensitive Applications

The reduced abrasiveness of Suregrip Soft Touch Velcro Tape ensures minimal wear and tear on fabrics while maintaining a secure hold. Whether you're a fashion designer working with high-end fabrics or in need of fastening solutions for healthcare and personal care products, Walonium’s Soft Touch tape offers the perfect balance of strength and softness. It’s also great for noise-sensitive environments, thanks to its quieter closure.
