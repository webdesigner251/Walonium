Bondix – Industrial-Grade Adhesive Tape

Heavy-Duty Fastening for Critical Applications

Bondix by Walonium is an industrial-grade adhesive hook & loop tape engineered to meet the toughest fastening demands in high-stress environments. Specifically designed for industries like HVAC, heavy machinery, electronics, automotive, and industrial assembly, Bondix offers exceptional bonding strength, long-term reliability, and easy application. Whether it's managing wires in a control panel or securing panels in HVAC systems, Bondix ensures components stay firmly in place—no compromise.

Contact us

Built to Withstand Stress, Heat & Time

With a pressure-sensitive, high-tack adhesive backing and premium hook & loop surface, Bondix delivers powerful hold on metal, plastic, wood, and painted surfaces. It resists vibrations, moisture, dust, and fluctuating temperatures—making it perfect for use in operational or rugged industrial zones. Its fast application, tool-free installation, and clean removability make it a preferred choice for technicians and engineers alike.

Specifications & Performance Highlights

  • Material: Nylon or polyester with industrial adhesive
  • Adhesive Type: High-tack acrylic (heat and solvent resistant)
  • Closure Strength: High tensile and shear strength
  • Cycle Life: 10,000+ openings/closings
  • Temperature Resistance: -20°C to 100°C
  • Cycle Life: 10,000+ fastening/unfastening cycles
  • Available Widths: 20mm to 100mm
  • Colors: Standard black and white, custom on request
  • Packaging: Rolls, die-cut shapes, or strips

  • Custom sizes and fast delivery
