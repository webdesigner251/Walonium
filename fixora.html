Fixora – Adhesive Hook & Loop Tape

Reliable Grip. Everyday Performance Walonium’s Adhesive Hook & Loop Tape offers a convenient, ready-to-use fastening solution that sticks to most clean, dry surfaces—no sewing or tools required. Designed for everyday applications, it’s perfect for retail displays, packaging, signage, cable management, workspace organization, DIY crafts, and general-purpose home or office use. Simply peel, stick, and press for an instant and reliable bond.

Contact us

Strong Adhesion with Reusability

Featuring an industrial-grade adhesive backing, this tape bonds securely to surfaces like plastic, metal, glass, and wood. It combines strong holding power with easy removability, allowing repeated open-close cycles without damaging the surface or leaving residue. Ideal for temporary or semi-permanent setups, the hook & loop mechanism ensures a tight grip while remaining flexible and reusable.

Specifications & Product Details

  • Material: Nylon or polyester with pressure-sensitive adhesive
  • Adhesive Type: High-tack rubber or acrylic (customizable)
  • Closure Strength: Medium to high, suitable for light to moderate loads
  • Cycle Life: 10,000+ openings/closings
  • Temperature Resistance: -10°C to 80°C
  • Available Widths: 16mm, 20mm, 25mm, 50mm (custom sizes available)
  • Color Options: Black, white (other colors on request)
  • Packaging: Roll format or pre-cut strips

  • Clean, reusable, and multi-surface friendly
